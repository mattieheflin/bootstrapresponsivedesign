1. Chick-fil-A

The Chick-fil-A chicken sandwich is a favorite of many, including expert Quang Hong who also loves their waffle fries. This classic chicken sandwich has been the most-ordered menu item at Chick-fil-A for (at least) two years running.

2. In-N-Out Burger

The menu is small, but the flavor is big at this favorite iconic West Coast burger chain. They use only fresh ingredients (they don’t even have freezers on-site), and customers know there’s also a not-so-secret menu. Anything can be ordered “Animal Style,” which means the patty is cooked with mustard and piled with grilled onions, pickles, and extra spread.

3. McDonald's

McDonald’s is nearly synonymous with the words “fast food,” so it’s no surprise that it's often the restaurant of choice for those seeking a satisfying menu and quick bite. Like many restaurants, they frequently offer special deals for those ordering through the McDonald’s app.

4. Wendy's

It’s hard to say if Wendy’s is known more for its burgers or its creamy frozen Frosty dessert. Customers return again and again for their iconic menu items, as well as new or limited-time fare like the grilled chicken wrap and the ghost pepper ranch chicken sandwich.

5. Raising Cane's

“You can count on Raising Cane's for freshly fried chicken finger meals with homestyle extras, like Texas toast, coleslaw, and fries," says Terrisa Meeks, 10 Best contributor. And fans know that Cane's Sauce is a necessary accompaniment to any meal.
